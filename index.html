Welcome to Theseus.

Theseus VR opens a whole new world of mobile computing capabilities. Environment too bright or too cramped for a laptop? Wish you had a multi-monitor station on the road? Not a problem! Using just a $4 Google Cardboard and the phone you already have, you can now work in places and ways that were previously unimaginable! Check out an article written about Theseus here.
